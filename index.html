Bet is a consonant and with that a very special one! Bet with a dot in it's belly is pronounced as a "b" as in "bees 🐝". Bet without a dot in it's belly is pronounced as a "v" as in "very".
As you will see reading without the nikud-punctuarion will require you to know the words to pronounce it properly.
